Privacy Policy for NexLingo

Effective Date: May 17, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to NexLingo. We deeply respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding your data when you use the NexLingo app.

2. Data Collection and Usage

We do not collect, store, or share any of your personal data. NexLingo operates purely as a local client tool on your device. We do not have user accounts, we do not use tracking cookies, and we do not integrate any advertising SDKs that collect user behavior.

3. API Key Security (BYOK)

NexLingo requires you to provide your own OpenAI API Key to function (Bring Your Own Key). Your API Key is encrypted and stored locally on your device using Apple's highly secure Keychain services. Your API Key is NEVER transmitted to or stored on any server owned by the developers of NexLingo. You can delete your API Key from your device at any time by clearing it in the app's Settings.

4. Third-Party Services

While we do not collect your data, the text you input for translation is sent directly from your device to OpenAI's API to generate the translated results. OpenAI's handling of your data is governed by their own privacy policy. Please note that OpenAI does not use data submitted via their API to train their models by default. You can review OpenAI's Privacy Policy here: https://openai.com/policies/privacy

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
