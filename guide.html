NexLingo User Guide

How to obtain and configure your personal OpenAI API Key for secure, private, and ultra-low-cost translation.

Step 1

Log in to the OpenAI Developer Platform

Please open your web browser and navigate to the official OpenAI developer platform:

Visit OpenAI Platform ↗

If you do not have an account yet, click "Sign up" to register, or simply use your Google or Apple account for a quick, authorized login.

Step 2

Create Your Exclusive API Key

Once you successfully log in, you will be directed to your API dashboard console:

  1. Locate and click the black [Create API key] button near the center or top-right of the dashboard.
  2. In the pop-up window, give your key a recognizable name (e.g., enter NexLingo in the "Name" field) so you know where it is being used.
  3. Click the [Create secret key] button at the bottom right.
Step 3

Copy and Save to the NexLingo App

After creating the key, a long string of unique characters starting with sk-... will appear on your screen. This is your API Key.

  1. Click the green [Copy] button next to it to copy it directly to your clipboard. (Note: For security reasons, this secret key is only displayed once. Please copy it immediately!)
  2. Open your NexLingo App on your iPhone and tap the gear icon to open the Settings page.
  3. Paste the copied API Key into the input field under "API Settings", and tap [Save & Apply].
🔒 Security & Privacy Commitment: Your API Key is fully encrypted and stored locally using Apple's highest hardware security standard, the Keychain. It is completely isolated and will NEVER be uploaded to any third-party or developer servers.
Step 4

Ensure Your OpenAI Account Has a Balance

NexLingo utilizes OpenAI's official model API, which operates on a strictly transparent "pay-as-you-go" basis. To ensure your translations process successfully, make sure your OpenAI account is funded:

⚡ Powered by GPT-4o-mini: NexLingo integrates OpenAI's cutting-edge gpt-4o-mini model. This state-of-the-art engine is blazing fast, highly intelligent at grasping context/emotions, and most importantly, unbelievably affordable (saving over 60% in costs compared to older legacy models like GPT-3.5).
  • We recommend an initial minimal top-up of $5.00 or $10.00 in your OpenAI billing dashboard.
  • Unbelievably Low Cost: Because NexLingo calls OpenAI's highly optimized, ultra-efficient language models, the translation cost per sentence is negligible. For regular daily communication, a $10.00 balance is typically more than enough to last you an entire year!
💡 Tip: You can set up "Usage limits" in your OpenAI account settings to prevent any unexpected usage or spending.
